Pike

Pike is the king of the Irish rivers, for many years it was my target number 1. They are typical ambush predators; they lie in wait for prey, holding perfectly still for long periods, and then exhibit remarkable acceleration as they strike. The pike grows to 20 kg, my personal record to 10 kg pike, caught on the suck river.
