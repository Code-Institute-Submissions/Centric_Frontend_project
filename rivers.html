River Brosna

"The River Brosna has a catchment area of 1,273km2 and is the 3rd largest tributary of the River Shannon. It rises in Lough Owel north of Mullingar and flows through Mullingar into Lough Ennell and south-west through the towns of Kilbeggan, Clara, and Ferbane to join the Shannon at Shannon harbour. This river was formerly one of the best trout rivers in Ireland and was also known as a salmon fishery in its own right."
