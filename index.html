"Midland Fishing "Ireland is recognised as being the outstanding fishing holiday destination in Europe. The vast variety and quality of fishing in Ireland makes it the perfect destination for your angling holiday. One of the interesting directions for fishing trips is midland, where you can visit some amazing rivers and catch record pike or amazing brown trout.
